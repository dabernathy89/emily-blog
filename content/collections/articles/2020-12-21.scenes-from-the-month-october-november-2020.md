<p>In typical 2020 fashion, I have ended the year with health issues. Six weeks ago, I woke up dizzy, and I&#8217;ve been that way ever since. The days have been long, but there have still been many good moments that I am happy to document here.</p>
<p>October and November are two of my three favorite months of the year, so I have lots of photos to share. As usual, most of them are of my babies.
