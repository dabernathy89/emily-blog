<p>We had such a great time with our family and friends in Texas over the holidays! We were blessed to be able to spend two weeks there&#8211;which is about the minimum amount of time we need to see each of our immediate families, extended family, and just a handful of our friends. We typically make it home only twice a year, so it is always special when we do.</p>
<p>Even though I didn&#8217;t take as many pictures during our trip as I had hoped to, I think I can give you a good glimpse of most of the highlights.
