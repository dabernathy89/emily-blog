<p>Immediately after I picked up Daniel&#8217;s brother from the airport, we made our way over to Nat&#8217;s stadium to watch the Washington National&#8217;s play along with a few of my friends from work. This was my first National&#8217;s baseball game. The weather was a bit chilly (especially up in our nosebleed section), but we had such a great time! It was definitely a fun DC experience.</p>
