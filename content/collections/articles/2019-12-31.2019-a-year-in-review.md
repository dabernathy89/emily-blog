<p>Happy New Year’s Eve! I hope everyone has had a wonderful Christmas season and has plans for an exciting New Year&#8217;s week. We got home on Sunday afternoon from a whirlwind Christmas trip to DFW, St. Petersburg, FL, and San Antonio, and we plan on having a very quiet New Year&#8217;s week at our house. So far, so good!</p>

I blogged very little in 2018 after the birth of my son and found myself unable to get back into any sort of routine or day-to-day life tasks until early 2019. I&#8217;m sad I didn&#8217;t get that posted &#8211; maybe I&#8217;ll do a retrospective look at 2018 at some point. But today I&#8217;m here to share a look at 2019!</p>
<p>2019 was such a fun year. I felt like I really learned how to be a mother and to be myself at the same time. We bought our first home. We did some traveling. I started blogging again. I started a position at work that has freed up my schedule and has allowed me to be a part-time working mom/part-time stay-at-home mom. Our baby boy had many milestones including learning to walk, turning one, starting daycare, getting a passport and traveling overseas, and starting to talk. Late in the year, we found out that we are expecting another baby boy in April 2020! Daniel, Carson, and I have just had such a blast in these early months of growing our little family.</p>
