<p>Welcome to my Scenes From the Week series! In these posts, I share the highlights from my previous week ranging from the simple, everyday things to the more memorable events. One of the primary reasons that I blog is to keep a scrapbook that I can look back on in the future, and these posts help serve that purpose.</p>

<p>(1) To kick off our weekend at home, we walked down to the Biscuits + Groovy food truck for breakfast. It was delicious, especially with a Top Chico to wash it down!</p>
<p>(2) Sunday we met up with our long-time friends Jordan and Kim and their baby girl. Their baby is so adorable. I loved holding her and watching her observe her surroundings. ðŸ™‚</p>
<p>(3) Said brunch (^) was at Bonhomie, a local French restaurant. I ordered the Croque Monsieur, and it was divine! The bread was thicker than other Croque Monsieur&#8217;s I&#8217;ve had in the past, which was delightful because I was able to soak up more of the cheese topping with it. ðŸ™‚</p>
<p>(4) Sunday night we grilled burgers in our apartment courtyard, ate Torchy&#8217;s chips &amp; queso, and relaxed. A perfect end to the weekend!</p>
<p>Not pictured:</p>
<ul>
<li>It was a great week for cooking at our house! We made some yummy weeknight meals. Our favorite: slow-cooker chicken burrito bowls from the <a href="http://amzn.to/2wH2YPx" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Skinnytaste Fast &amp; Slow</a> cookbook.</li>

<li>A Saturday morning/ early afternoon full of cleaning and organizing</li>
