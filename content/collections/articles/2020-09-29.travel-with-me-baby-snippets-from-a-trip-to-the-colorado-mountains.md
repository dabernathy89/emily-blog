<p>I have so many trips that I&#8217;d love to create <a href="https://www.everydayaccountsblog.com/category/travel-with-me/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Travel with Me</a> posts for, many of which were several years ago. There were a few years there where we were able to make travel a big priority, but I couldn&#8217;t keep up with the posts on top of my full-time job. Then we started having our beautiful babies, and we weren&#8217;t traveling much, but there wasn&#8217;t as much time to write. This is one of those trips. In July 2019, we took our then-one-year-old Carson to Colorado. The main reason for the trip was a friend&#8217;s wedding, but we added on a few days to enjoy the mountains together as a family.</p>

We stayed in an Airbnb in the cozy mountain town of Dillon. The Airbnb appears to not be available anymore, which is too bad. It was a great place for a small family. We rented the larger baby equipment from <a href="https://babysaway.com/locations/denver-co/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Baby&#8217;s Away of Denver</a> &#8211; I share my thoughts about and experiences renting baby equipment in <a href="https://www.everydayaccountsblog.com/2019/08/baby-travel-gear-packing-vs-renting/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">this blog post</a>. Traveling with a baby is hard enough, so we have often made it a little easier on ourselves by renting some of the equipment.</p>
