<p>I know I&#8217;ve said it over and over on the blog already, but I had the best Thanksgiving week. This is more like a full week re-cap because Thanksgiving break started for us last Tuesday night. We drove out to Harpers Ferry that evening after work to meet my parents and brother. Immediately, the games began! The favorite game of the week was Ticket to Ride.</p>

<p>Friday we packed up and drove into Northern Virginia. We had one last meal all together, and then my parents and brother went to the airport for their flight home. My sisters got to stay one more day, so we filled the rest of Friday night and Saturday with some great meals out, The Theory of Everything (fantastic movie by the way), and lots of Gilmore Girls at home.</p>
<p>Saturday evening Daniel and I went to Barnes &amp; Noble (our office) to get some work done. We took the picture below to say goodbye to this B&amp;N location since it was probably our last time to go there &#8211; we move to Texas in two weeks!</p>
