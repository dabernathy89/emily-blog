<p>We have an orange cat named Banana. He is a furry little ball of joy that I look forward to going home to everyday (along with my husband, of course!). We adopted him a little over a year ago, and he came to us with the name Banana. At first, we were hesitant and wanted to change his name, but then it grew on us. Now I absolutely love how his name makes people chuckle, and it just seems to suit him in an unexplainable way.</p>
<p>Daniel and I take pictures of Banana all the time. He never ceases to crack us up with his funny poses and adorable faces. He acts much like a dog &#8211; begging for cuddles and people food, waiting at the door for us to come home from work, and always striving to be the center of attention. Â He is also quite fat and loves to eat plastic and lick windows. He is a strange one. But we love him!</p>
<p>I decided that I&#8217;d like to share a glimpse into the life of Banana with a monthly series called &#8220;Lazy Days with Banana&#8221;. While these posts won&#8217;t entertain everyone (feel free to click away), I think that these photos may provide amusement for some. Welcome to Banana&#8217;s little world!</p>
