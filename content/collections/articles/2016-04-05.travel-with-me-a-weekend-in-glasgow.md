<h3>Day 2</h3>
<p>Glasgow is a huge city, so with only two days to explore we knew we wanted to do some sort of bus tour. We picked the <span style="text-decoration: underline;">City Sightseeing Hop On-Hop Off Bus tour</span>. These are the big red tourist buses that you will see in pretty much every major city. The major benefit of using these buses is that your ticket pays for both a tour of the city as well as your transportation for that day. You can hop off whenever you&#8217;d like, and when you hop back on you can resume the audio tour right where you left off.Â Â I&#8217;m not a huge fan of tours that don&#8217;t have an actual person speaking to you, but it was a decent tour.</p>
