<p>I could not think of a great blog post idea for today, so I thought I&#8217;d share with you my favorite TV shows that I&#8217;m currently watching. Yes, I know I watch way too much TV, but I will say that I don&#8217;t keep up with these shows on live TV (I go through them very slowly on Hulu and Netflix), and I am almost always doing something productive while I&#8217;m watching my shows (getting ready, working on the blog, doing Project Life, folding clothes, etc.). There have been times when I&#8217;ve thought maybe I should cut down on the number of shows I watch, but I&#8217;ve decided that I don&#8217;t need to do that until I find myself wasting away in front of a screen for hours every night and not getting anything else done.</p>
<h2>Parenthood</h2>
<p>This is my #1 favorite show that I&#8217;m watching right now. In fact, it&#8217;s probably my second favorite of all time &#8211; right behind Gilmore Girls. It will make you laugh and cry and bond with the characters in a way that just makes it seem so real.</p>
<p><a href="https://images.everydayaccountsblog.com/wp-content/uploads//2014/03/parenthood_s5_go_1920x1080_post_v3.jpg"><img loading="lazy" alt="Parenthood_s5_GO_1920x1080_post_v3" src="https://images.everydayaccountsblog.com/wp-content/uploads//2014/03/parenthood_s5_go_1920x1080_post_v3.jpg?w=775" width="465" height="261" /></a></p>
<h2>Friday Night Lights</h2>
<p>I&#8217;ve talked about this show on the blog before, but I just love it! It falls right behind Parenthood in the rankings. It reminds Daniel and me of growing up in small town Texas. We have been watching all of the seasons over the past year, and we are just about the wrap up the whole series.</p>
<p><a href="https://images.everydayaccountsblog.com/wp-content/uploads//2014/03/friday_night_lights.jpg"><img loading="lazy" alt="friday_night_lights" src="https://images.everydayaccountsblog.com/wp-content/uploads//2014/03/friday_night_lights.jpg" width="325" height="432" /></a></p>
<h2>Scandal</h2>
<p>The story line of this show just keeps getting more and more twisted and crazy! I love Kerry Washington too. It gets too graphic for me at some points but not so much that I can&#8217;t watch the show. Lots of crazy stuff goes on in the White House in the show &#8211; makes you wonder what really goes on behind the scenes in the real White House.</p>
<p><a href="https://images.everydayaccountsblog.com/wp-content/uploads//2014/03/scandal.jpg"><img loading="lazy" alt="scandal" src="https://images.everydayaccountsblog.com/wp-content/uploads//2014/03/scandal.jpg" width="318" height="159" /></a></p>
<h2>Law &amp; Order SVU</h2>
<p>I&#8217;ve watched this for a long time, and I still really enjoy it. I miss Detective Stabler, but as long as they keep Olivia Benson around, I think I will keep watching.</p>
