You took us on the <a href="http://www.everydayaccountsblog.com/2015/12/hello-friends-a-big-announcement/" target="_blank">most epic adventure of a lifetime</a> at a much earlier age than I could have imagined (see all related posts <a href="http://www.everydayaccountsblog.com/tag/abernathy-adventure/" target="_blank">here</a>, if you are interested) .
