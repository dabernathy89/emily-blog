We had really been looking forward to this day because we had heard so many great things about this train ride from our friends.Â I&#8217;m so happy we chose to travel from Puno to Cusco by rail because we were able to look out the window at various parts of the Peruvian countryside that we wouldn&#8217;t have seen otherwise and chat with some fellow travelers.</p>
<p>Since we had purchased our Peru Rail tickets for the <a href="http://www.perurail.com/andean-explorer" target="_blank">Andean Explorer</a> in advance, the check-in process was a breeze. Once we arrived at the station, we just had to wait in a short line to get our tickets and passports verified, and then we had a short 15 minute wait before boarding.</p>
