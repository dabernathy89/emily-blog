<p>Welcome to my Scenes From the Month series! Â In these posts, I share the highlights from my previous month ranging from the simple, everyday things to the more memorable events. One of the primary reasons that I blog is to keep a scrapbook that I can look back on in the future, and these posts help serve that purpose. Now that I have babies, everyday moments are extra special!</p>
<p>March opened up with my grandmother&#8217;s funeral. We got together as a family to celebrate her life, and it was really special. She will be missed dearly.</p>
<p>We ended the month with our first &#8220;family of four&#8221; vacation! We drove to Port Aransas for a long beach weekend. While it wasn&#8217;t the super sunny weather we&#8217;d imagined for a beach trip, we had a great time. My in-laws joined, so Daniel and I got a date night out too!</p>
<h2>Favorites from the Month:</h2>
<p><em>{This section contains some Amazon affiliate links.}</em></p>
<p><strong>Fiction Book</strong> &#8211; <a href="https://amzn.to/3sL6UeB" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Majesty</a> by Katharine McGee. This is the second book in the American Royals series. I really enjoyed it! I like the characters, and the plot line is so interesting. The series is about the modern-day American royal family. Such an interesting fictional world to follow.</p>
<p><strong>Nonfiction Book</strong> &#8211; <a href="https://amzn.to/31FqT2H" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Unfinished</a> by Priyanka Chopra Jonas. I really, really enjoyed this book. What an interesting woman! I knew nothing about her before reading this book, but I love hearing about her life, her entry into the US acting world, her incredible work ethic, and her story of meeting Nick Jonas, falling in love, and getting married.</p>
<p><strong>Music</strong> &#8211; Still on a Pandora kick. My latest favorite station is the &#8220;Happy&#8221; station. Such a fun mix of happy tunes!</p>
<p><strong>Movie/TV</strong> &#8211; I&#8217;ve really enjoyed the new episodes of &#8220;This is Us&#8221; this month, especially the scenes on the early days of parenting. I can relate so much, and the character development and dialogue in that show is just beautiful.</p>
<p><strong>Item</strong> &#8211; Our Peloton. Does that count as an item? I&#8217;m finally getting in some workouts during the week, and it actually seems to be helping with my dizzy symptoms. It&#8217;s hard to have an excuse when I can literally get a 30 minute spin workout in without leaving my house. I&#8217;m also enjoying the yoga and stretching classes on the Peloton app.</p>
<p><strong>Activity</strong> &#8211; Our beach trip was pretty special. It was the first time both boys went to the beach, and they had a blast. It was not a relaxing trip, but watching them play in the sand and the waves with such joy made it all worth it.</p>
<p><strong>Accomplishment</strong> &#8211; Shopping for and planting our new plant babies in the backyard. This probably doesn&#8217;t sound like a big accomplishment to most people, but we have never planted our own plants before. I feel super proud, and I hope they survive and thrive!</p>
