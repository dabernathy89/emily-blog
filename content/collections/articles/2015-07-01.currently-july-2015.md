<p><strong>Craving</strong> | An adventure! Good thing we head to Peru exactly one month from today! I can hardly wait!</p>
<p><strong>Grilling</strong> |<i> </i> Salmon burgers. Last night, we sat out by the pool and grilled salmon burgers. I love that we have such a nice area here in the middle of the city to do things like this in the fresh air. Despite not being a hot weather lover, I am really enjoying most things about the summer this year!</p>
<p><b>Listening </b>| To the new Mumford and Sons album Wilder Mind on repeat at work.</p>
<p><strong>Planning</strong> | All kinds of exciting things right now including lots and lots of travel!</p>
<p><strong>Decorating</strong> | My Project Life album. I have recently been getting back into setting up my 2014 album. I&#8217;m really behind, but I&#8217;m catching up quickly!</p>
<p><strong>Reading</strong> | <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Daring-Greatly-Courage-Vulnerable-Transforms/dp/1592408419" target="_blank">Daring Greatly</a> and <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Luckiest-Girl-Alive-Jessica-Knoll-ebook/dp/B00LD1OITO/ref=sr_1_1?s=books&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1435714945&amp;sr=1-1&amp;keywords=luckiest+girl+alive" target="_blank">Luckiest Girl Alive</a>. I have <a href="http://www.everydayaccountsblog.com/2015/06/summer-reading-list/" target="_blank">a lot more</a> that I&#8217;d like to read this summer, so I&#8217;m trying to read a lot more each day.</p>
<p><strong>Drinking</strong> | New coffee. I picked up some coffee beans from my favorite local coffee shop here in Austin called Summer Moon. They wood-fire roast their own coffee beans, and it is delicious! We have had a lot of fun grinding our own beans and making coffee in a french press whenever we have time.</p>
