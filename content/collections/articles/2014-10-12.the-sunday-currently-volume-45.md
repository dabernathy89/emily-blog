<p>Happy Sunday! We had another mostly free weekend that we filled up with exploring the city, relaxing, and getting errands and chores done. I had a bit of work I had to do over the weekend as well, but let&#8217;s just focus on the good stuff from the weekend!</p>
<p>Friday we did our usual dinner out and came home to lounge around and get to bed at a decent hour.</p>
<p>Saturday was fun. We slept in and then made our way into the city for the day. We went to the Taste of DC 2014 Food Festival where we tried lots of food samples from different DC restaurants.</p>
