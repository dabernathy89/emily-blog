<p>Last Saturday, Spring was in the air in DC. It was a beautiful 65 degrees, and we decided it would be a shame to stay indoors working all day. So we made plans to meet good friends at Union Market in DC for a long lunch. We were especially eager to get out because Snowstorm Wiley was on his way to meet us the following Monday morning.</p>
<p><a href="https://images.everydayaccountsblog.com/wp-content/uploads//2014/03/20140320-224410.jpg"><img alt="20140320-224410.jpg" src="https://images.everydayaccountsblog.com/wp-content/uploads//2014/03/20140320-224410.jpg" /></a></p>
<p>We didn&#8217;t actually realize that the market was indoors, but it was still a great way to enjoy the weather because they opened up all the entryways to let the fresh air in. The market is made up of a variety of food stands, a few cute shops, and one sit-down restaurant, all in a big warehouse-type building.</p>
<p><a href="https://images.everydayaccountsblog.com/wp-content/uploads//2014/03/20140320-223927.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-full" alt="20140320-223927.jpg" src="https://images.everydayaccountsblog.com/wp-content/uploads//2014/03/20140320-223927.jpg" /></a></p>
<p>The flower stand had some really pretty options that made me even more excited for Spring!</p>
<p><a href="https://images.everydayaccountsblog.com/wp-content/uploads//2014/03/20140320-223938.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-full" alt="20140320-223938.jpg" src="https://images.everydayaccountsblog.com/wp-content/uploads//2014/03/20140320-223938.jpg" /></a></p>

<p>One of the shops inside the market, Salt and Sundry, sold all types of homegood items along with stationery, craft items, and a fabulous candle selection. It reminded me a lot of Anthropologie without the clothes (unfortunately, it was priced the same as well).</p>
<p><a href="https://images.everydayaccountsblog.com/wp-content/uploads//2014/03/20140320-224128.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-full" alt="20140320-224128.jpg" src="https://images.everydayaccountsblog.com/wp-content/uploads//2014/03/20140320-224128.jpg" /></a></p>
<p>We enjoy brunch at Bidwell Restaurant. It was a long wait but that is expected for brunch on a Saturday.</p>
<p><a href="https://images.everydayaccountsblog.com/wp-content/uploads//2014/03/20140320-224143.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-full" alt="20140320-224143.jpg" src="https://images.everydayaccountsblog.com/wp-content/uploads//2014/03/20140320-224143.jpg" /></a></p>
