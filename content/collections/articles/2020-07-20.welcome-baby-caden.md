https://images.everydayaccountsblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/07/IMG_5169-600x800.jpg 600w" sizes="(max-width: 768px) 100vw, 768px" /></a>Baby Caden  Stuart was born two weeks early on April 6, 2020 at 8:46pm, weighing 8 lbs, 7 oz.  It was a very different experience having a hospital delivery during the pandemic. The birth was very different from my first &#8211; Caden was induced and the epidural didn&#8217;t work all the way (a little scary), and it was just me and Daniel at the hospital for two days. With Carson, there were no delivery scares or complications, and all of our immediate family was there at the hospital to welcome the baby into the world after he was born. Maybe I&#8217;ll share Caden&#8217;s labor and delivery story another time. Regardless, everything ended up fine, and baby is healthy and perfect. He instantly filled a space in our family that we didn&#8217;t know existed.</p>
<p>Long before Caden was born, I had booked a &#8220;Fresh 48&#8221; session to get photos of our family in the hospital, but we obviously had to cancel that session. We found out while we were at the hospital that there was an onsite newborn photographer, so we took full advantage and had an impromptu session! I wish our sweet Carson could have been in the photos, but we plan to do a family photo session in a few months. Here are a few of my favorites:</p>
