<h2>Favorites from the Month:</h2>
<p><strong>Fiction Book</strong> &#8211; <a href="https://amzn.to/3ruuxqT" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><em>The Testaments</em></a> by Margaret Atwood. This is the sequel to <em>A Handmaid&#8217;s Tale</em>, and I really enjoyed the story. It&#8217;s not as good as the first, but I loved the deeper look into the characters, some who are in the first book and some who are not. Also I&#8217;m really looking forward to the next season of the show on Hulu.</p>
<p><strong>Nonfiction Book</strong> &#8211; I read several nonfiction books this month. My favorite was <a href="https://amzn.to/3cOitwD" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><em>Everything is Figureoutable</em></a> by Marie Forleo. While I don&#8217;t agree with everything Marie had to say, I felt very inspired by this book and am ready to take on and &#8220;figure out&#8221; several ideas I&#8217;ve had floating around in my mind for years.</p>
<p><strong>Music</strong> &#8211; I&#8217;m still very into <a href="https://amzn.to/3cOiDnJ" target="_blank" rel="noopener">folklore</a> and <a href="https://amzn.to/3rwOw8g" target="_blank" rel="noopener">evermore</a>. Also, I&#8217;m in love with Ed Sheeran&#8217;s new song &#8220;<a href="https://amzn.to/2YWZzLT" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Afterglow</a>&#8220;.</p>
<p><strong>Movie/TV</strong> &#8211; The Crown (Netflix). We are finishing up Season 4.</p>
<p><strong>Item</strong> &#8211; I gave Daniel this for Christmas, but it has ended up being more of a gift for myself. Oops! It is the <a href="https://fromourplace.com/products/always-essential-cooking-pan?gclid=Cj0KCQiA34OBBhCcARIsAG32uvMKsYPSr2OqYZznpo9heXupHZl2g03omR85e2LN5TcVG1YmYJ6COVsaAmUzEALw_wcB" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Always Pan</a> by Our Place. It&#8217;s so beautiful, easy to clean, and fun to cook lots of different things with!</p>
<p><strong>Activity</strong> &#8211; Staycation in Austin. My parents took the kiddos for a weekend, and we enjoyed some of our favorite restaurants (outside) and walked around the new part of South Congress. Lots of fun and rest and relaxation!</p>
<p><strong>Accomplishment</strong> &#8211; Potty training Carson! He&#8217;s not 100% potty trained, but we have made a lot of progress!
