<p>(2) My brother is a student at Baylor, and he lives in the same residence hall that Daniel and I lived in. You can&#8217;t see it without zooming in, but there are several photos of Daniel and me and many of our friends in here. <p>(3) Sunday morning we decided to go to the late service at church, so we had time to get a brunch plate to share at one of our favorite local coffee shops, Patika. We stayed there a couple hours to get some work things done (blogging for me, freelance project for Daniel) before heading over to church.</p>
<p>(4) For lunch on Sunday we went to a new-to-us local Indian restaurant, New India, where I had a delicious chicken tikka masala.</p>
