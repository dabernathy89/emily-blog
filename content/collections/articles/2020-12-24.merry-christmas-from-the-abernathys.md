Merry Christmas from Daniel, me, Carson (2.5 years), and Caden (8 months)! May you have a beautiful holiday with loved ones. We will be soaking up all the coziness, rest, and Christmas cheer with my family. I love celebrating the birth of Christ with loved ones every year.  More than ever this year, I need to remind myself that Jesus is who we are celebrating in the midst of all the extra fluff of the season. We have so much to be thankful and joyful for, even during difficult times such as 2020.</p>
<p>We had a very special photo shoot around Caden&#8217;s 6 month birthday this October, so I wanted to share those photos here as well as our Christmas card above.</p>
<p>Thank you to <a href="http://www.snowpixiephotography.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Snow Pixie Photography</a> here in Austin for the beautiful photos. We had originally scheduled a Fresh 48 session with her at the hospital for right after Caden&#8217;s birth, but since he was born April 6, 2020, we had to cancel that for obvious reasons. Instead, we were able to postpone to a fall family photo shoot. I will treasure these photos forever.</p>
