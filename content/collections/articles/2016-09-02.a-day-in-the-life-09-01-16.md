</a>One of my favorite types of blog posts to read are &#8220;day in the life&#8221; posts where readers get to walk through the details of the blogger&#8217;s day. I thought I&#8217;d share one of those on my blog today! I couldn&#8217;t remember if I had done one of these before, but I just found <a href="http://www.everydayaccountsblog.com/2014/01/a-photo-an-hour-my-everyday-life/" target="_blank">one I did in January 2014</a> when we lived in DC, and I had recently started blogging. I laughed out loud when I read it because it is crazy how similar my routine is over two and a half years later!</p>
<p>Here is what a typical weekday is for me in September:</p>
<p>At about 5:20am, I roll out of bed (after snoozing several times), throw on work out clothes, and wash my face. I like to get up early on weekdays to get my workout in and have a slow morning before work.</p>

I also pour myself a big bowl of cereal &#8211; I wish I made myself a nice, nutritious breakfast every morning, but I just eat cereal each morning. At this point, it&#8217;s usually about 7:15, so I have forty-five minutes to read <a href="http://www.theskimm.com/" target="_blank">TheSkimm</a> and some blog posts, update <a href="https://www.youneedabudget.com/" target="_blank">YNAB</a> (our budgeting tool), and do whatever else I want on the computer while eating breakfast and drinking my cup of coffee.</p>
