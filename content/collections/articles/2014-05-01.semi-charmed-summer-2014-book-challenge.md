<p>I have been thinking about doing one of the book challenges held by <a href="http://megancstroup.blogspot.com/" target="_blank">Megan at Semi-Charmed Kind of Life</a> for a while now. My biggest hesitation is that I enjoy spontaneously choosing my next book to read based on what I feel like reading rather than having them planned out. Also, I typically cannot read as fast as I will need to in order to finish the number of books required in the challenge. However, I think that participating in one of these challenges will help me to read faster, and I think that the group effort will be a fun way to link up with other bloggers who love to read. So, I&#8217;m going to give it a shot. If it doesn&#8217;t work out, that&#8217;s fine, but I want to see if it&#8217;s something that I like.  If this sounds like something you would want to participate it, click <a href="http://megancstroup.blogspot.com/2014/04/semi-charmed-summer-2014-book-challenge.html" target="_blank">here</a> to see the rules and other information on Megan&#8217;s blog. You should also click around to see some of her other posts &#8211; she is one of my favorite bloggers!</p>
