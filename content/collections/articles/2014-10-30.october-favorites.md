<h3><strong>Ulta Professional Vampire Kiss</strong></h3>
<p>I have been having a lot fun painting my nails the past couple of months. I&#8217;m still very new to it, but I&#8217;m getting better, and in addition to purchasing a few new options, I&#8217;m reaching for colors I already have but have not used much. I already had this berry color &#8211; it was given to me by my friend Shannon for us bridesmaids to wear at her wedding. I put it on a couple of weeks ago for the first time since the wedding in June, and I love it! It seems to have a much higher staying power than the Essie or Sephora X polishes I have tried, but about the same as OPI. The color is beautiful and great for the fall/winter season!</p>
<h3><strong>Maybelline FIT Me Concealer</strong></h3>
<p>Ever since I started wearing makeup, I have used the Mary Kay concealer under my eyes. I still think that concealer works great, but I was getting really tired of having to contact a Mary Kay specialist and arrange to pick it up from them. So, I decided to try out a new drugstore concealer, and I&#8217;m very impressed by this one! It works just as well as the Mary Kay product, and it is a great price. I highly recommend it.</p>
<h2>Body Care</h2>
<h3><strong>Bath &amp; Body Works Sweet Cinnamon Pumpkin Body Lotion</strong></h3>
<p>This is my second year to use this lotion. I love how it reminds me of my love of the fall season. It smells like pumpkin bread, cinnamon, and apple cider in one bottle! I know this is going to continue being an annual favorite.</p>
<h2>Candle</h2>
<h3>Bath &amp; Body Works Autumn Candle</h3>
<p>This candle smells amazing! I don&#8217;t really know how to describe it, but my husband and I both think it smells slightly Christmas-y. It is a great blend of fall and Christmas scents &#8211; perfect for enjoying the current season and anticipating the magic of the Christmas season! Plus the packaging of the candle is beautiful and makes a great fall decoration.</p>
