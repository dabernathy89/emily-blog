<h2>Cliffs of Moher</h2>
<p>Many people think of the Cliffs of Moher as the primary stop on an Ireland road trip, and rightfully so. One could easily spend several hours sitting and gazing at the dramatic drop into the Atlantic Ocean, letting your mind rest and wander (weather permitting). We were apparently very lucky to have a sunny day at the Cliffs during winter, so if visiting the Cliffs is your main goal of an Irish road trip, I&#8217;d recommend going at a different time of year, but it worked out for us.</p>
