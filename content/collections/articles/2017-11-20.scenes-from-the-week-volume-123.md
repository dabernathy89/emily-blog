<p>(2) The new Central Public Library in downtown Austin is fabulous.Â  It was a $125 million project that was approved over seven years ago. It has mostly been well-received by Austinites. It is a truly gorgeous space with plenty of outlet-equipped window seats to work on your laptop with a city or river view, a rooftop garden to sit and enjoy a book with a view (view from the downtown-facing side shown here), and a wonderful children&#8217;s section that I can&#8217;t wait to take Baby Boy to in the future! The design of the interior and exterior spaces is tasteful and impressive too.</p>
<p>(3) This is the view from the rooftop garden into the library down the various floors. The view from the bottom floor up is very cool &#8211; the series of staircases look straight out of Harry Potter. It&#8217;s gorgeous!</p>
