My very well-loved boots

Yes, these boots look pretty worn out, but I wore them every single day for nearly three months. While real leather isn’t made to go through all of the snow and rain that we experienced (resulting in the wear and tear pictured above), the quality did allow the boot to stay in good walking condition throughout the whole three months, and I don’t think fake leather shoes would have lasted.
