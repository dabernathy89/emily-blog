I’m Back!

August 12, 2019 · by Emily

Hello, hello! After a break from regular posting for over a year, I am so ready to get back into this wonderful community, both as a reader and as a writer. Life is very different for me now, but at the same time, I still hold very similar interests and values to what I held when I shared my first post in 2013. You can expect a weekly post from me with topics ranging from everyday life updates, motherhood, and travel, including tips for traveling with a baby. In the meantime, I thought I’d catch you up on some of our life highlights during 2018 and 2019.

  • We had our first baby, Carson Joel Abernathy, on April 24, 2018. I announced his birth in this post and shared his six month photos in this one. He is now 15 months old!
  • I took a seven month maternity leave that I am so grateful for. I shared my thoughts in this post before returning to work part-time at the end of November 2018.
  • Carson has had so many firsts already that I am not able to list them all, but here are a few –
    • First trip – Fredericksburg for Daniel’s first Father’s Day in June 2018
    • First time on an airplane – Chicago in July 2018
    • First Halloween – 
    • First Thanksgiving –
    • First time pulling up to standing – Christmas Eve Eve 2018
    • First Christmas – We were in New Mexico in the snowy mountains with my family.
    • First steps – January 23, 2019 (9 months)
    • First time officially walking – February 26, 2019
    • First Easter –
    • First birthday –
    • First day of daycare – July 31, 2019 (We had a part-time nanny before.)
  • We dedicated Carson at our church on November 11, 2018 in front of our church congregation and family.
  • We purchased our first house in February 2019 and have so enjoyed our first few months settling in and making it a home.
  • Carson turned ONE (cue all the tears!) in April 2019. I had a big party for him with a Winnie the Pooh theme and lots of friends and family.
  • We took family photos for Carson’s birthday.  
  • We have done a fair amount of travel since Carson was born. Below is a list of where we’ve traveled, not including some local trips, visits to my parents’ and in-laws’ homes, and work trips:
    • Chicago in July 2018 (Carson’s first flight)
    • Nashville in August 2018
    • San Diego in September 2018 (first baby-free trip)
    • New Mexico for Christmas with my family in December 2018
    • Atlanta in May 2019 to visit my cousin’s family
    • Savannah and Hilton Head in May 2019 for our 8th anniversary (baby-free)
    • NYC in July 2019 (baby-free)
    • Colorado in July 2019 for a wedding

Those are the highlights! I look forward to sharing and interacting with those who followed along before as well as anyone who may be new. Going forward, be sure to check back on Mondays for posts about motherhood, travel, and more! I’ll be “talking” with you soon.

{Thanks for reading! Want to know a little more about me? I’m a CPA learning how to balance a career with motherhood and regular travel while planting roots at home in the Austin, TX area. When I’m not exploring my home city with my husband Daniel and baby boy Carson or cuddling with my chubby orange cat Banana, I’m probably either out on a playdate with my son or planning, packing for, traveling to, or daydreaming about our next adventure. After all, my travel mug collection is never complete. If you have wanderlust too, be sure to follow me on Instagram (and InstaStories!), Pinterest, and Facebook, or subscribe to my blog!}
